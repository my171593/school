Welcome to The Elms Jr. Middle School, a community of learning that features Kindergarten to Grade 8, located within the richly diverse Albion/Islington community. With a legacy of over 50 years of educational excellence, we are continually engaged in the school improvement process to meet the constantly changing needs of 21st Century Learners. As a member of Model Schools for the Inner-city within the Toronto District School Board, we are fortunate to have additional resources and a mandate to be the hub of our community, offering an extensive range of supports, including dental, vison and enhanced community partnerships. We share our site with the City of Toronto pool and recreational facilities, the EarlyON Child and Family Centre, a private daycare and an after-school Boys and Girls Club.
